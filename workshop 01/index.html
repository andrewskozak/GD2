Emilie Villeneuve

Interviewed by Andrew Kozak

What music do you like to listen to while working?
To be honest, i like to listen to pop. I don't really need "locked in" music.


Name three designers whose work you find inspiring.

  • Bruno Munari.
  • Elliot Earls.
  • Yayoi Kusama.


Name something (a format, an item) you want to learn how to design but haven't had the chance to try to yet.
I guess I haven't done book covers, or publications, or magazines. Or a product. or stickers. Anything kind of fun.


If you were a font, you'd be (you can't say helvetica or comic sans-- both jokes are played). Explain why?
The first one that comes to mind is Calibri. It's kind of cute, fun, neutral.


Name one website that you rely on (try to avoid a platform like google/amazon/pinterest/etc).
Can I say Canva? Adobe Fonts? Can I switch to Spotify?


Do you have a minor at OCAD?
I thought I would like to get into Digital Futures, but it wasn't for me. I'm kind of an indecisive person. I'd probably do printmaking if I could.
